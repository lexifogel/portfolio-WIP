Mobalytics: Externship Work

Timeline: 1 semester

Role: Market Research and Data Analytics extern

Skills: Market research & analysis, Landscape mapping, Excel

Project description: This externship tasked me with conducting a landscape analysis of the nascent and constantly evolving play-to-earn crypto market; particularly, P2E games and companion tools used to help gamers improve their performance.

After two onboarding assignments, my work was broken down into three main projects. All deliverables are attached in the appendix.

Onboarding | Market Research | Data Analytics | Synthesis | Appendix

Onboarding

E-Sports

  • My first order of business in this role was to establish familiarity with the market!
  • I spent 3 full days immersing myself in e-sports history and developing an understanding of the primary games and developers within the space.

Web3

  • Next, I spent a week decomposing Web3; I learned about blockchain technology, crypto, decentralized transactions, and the implications of each of those for e-sports moving forwards.
  • I learned that use of blockchain technologies with cryptocurrencies have led to the birth of a new genre of games: play-to-earn (P2E), the focus of my projects.

Business Models & Fee Structures

  • Finally, I had an overview of typical business models seen within the P2E industry. This was a prelude to case studies conducted in the first project to dissect the profitability and organization of specific companies.

Industry & Mobalytics Overview

    To solidify my new understanding of the e-sports, web3, crypto, and P2E markets, I created a mind map visualizing main players within and influences on the industry.

    • Deliverable: Market landscape, Appendix A

    I also created a fact sheet highlighting key information about Mobalytics.

    • Deliverable: Mobalytics fact sheet, Appendix B

Market Research

After establishing a pretty good idea of the landscape of P2E games, it became time to zoom in on some specific P2E games and publishers. I looked at 12 innovative, newer games within the space and conducted thorough case studies on each.

Case studies completed

  • 9D NFT, Appendix C1
  • Netvrk, Appendix C2
  • Gala Games: Spider Tanks
  • Luna Rush
  • Prometheus
  • PlayMining: Cookin' Burger
  • DOSE: Dustland Runner
  • CryptoMines Reborn
  • Dragonary
  • Velhalla
  • Komoverse
  • MAD Metaverse

Data Analytics

Next, I conducted correlation and regression analyses on a dataset of industry company information to answer a given set of questions. I documented my findings with an analytics report.

  • Deliverable: Analytics report, Appendix D

Synthesis

Finally, I made a final presentation to company leaders explaining my insight, recommendations and rationale.

  • Deliverable: Final report, Appendix E
